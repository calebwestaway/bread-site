Honk Shortcut

You need an Apple device to use this!

This needs the Shortcuts app to work. It should come pre-installed, but you can redownload it if you deleted it

1. Click here

2. Click Get Shortcut

3. Click Add Shortcut. You can now use the shorcut from this app to Honk, but if you want it on your home screen, read on

4. Hold down on your shiny new shortcut until the menu appears

5. Click Share

6. Scroll down and click Add to Home Screen

7. Click Add

8. Press the Honk Button!
